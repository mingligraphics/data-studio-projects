An International Students' Guide to Find H-1B Visa Sponsorships in U.S.

H-1B visa is the largest temporary employment-based visa program in the United States. It enables employers to hire highly skilled foreign workers. Every year, over 200,000 international students are looking for jobs that sponsor H-1b visa, which has a annual cap of 85,000.
